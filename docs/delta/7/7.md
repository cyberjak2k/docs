???- faq "FAQ"

    **Q:  Why are cover connectors designed this way?**

    **A:** In order to ensure high degree of freedom and reproducibility, all printed parts can be printed on the machine, so the design is disassembled, and it will be easier to print and maintain after splitting.


    **Q: Can I seal the box?**

    **A:** Yes, it is necessary to add additional materials and a small number of changes to achieve the sealing of the box, without losing any printing size, I will release it after improvement.
