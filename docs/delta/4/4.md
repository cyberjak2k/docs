## 0.Precautions

!!! warning "warning"
    When fastening the printed parts with screws，do not tighten them too much to avoid damaging the printed parts.


!!! info "info"
    Printed parts throughout this section make use of threaded inserts, these need to be pressed into the printed part with a soldering iron:
    ![image.webp](1.webp){.img1}

## 1.Limit assembly

### Required materials

1.Photoelectric limit switch * 3
   ![image.webp](xwkg.webp){.img1}

2.M3*8 countersunk screws * 12
   ![image.webp](M3X8.webp){.img1}

3.T-Nut type 20 - M3 * 12
   ![image.webp](20M3.webp){.img1}

### Printed parts

1.[Limit swtich mount] * 3
[Limit swtich mount]: dy1.stl
   ![image.webp](dy1.webp){.img1}

### Assembly process

1. Insert the photoelectric limit switch into the limit switch mount, and and secure it with two M3*8 countersunk screws, as shown in the figure.
   ![image.webp](2.webp){.img1}

!!! info "info"
    Do not overtighten to avoid damaging the printed part.

2. Pull the connecting wire out from the right side of the limit swtich mount to prevent the stepper motor from pressing on the wire during installation.
   ![image.webp](3.webp){.img1}

3. Use two M3*8 countersunk screws to pass through the limit swtich mount, and screw on two t-nuts type 20-M3, as shown in the picture.
   ![image.webp](4.webp){.img1}

!!! info "info"
    Do not tighten them completely, just thread them on to the screws.

4. Secure the limit swtich mount to the frame. During installation, ensure the protrusion of the metal connecting slider is centered on the photoelectric sensor of the photoelectric limit switch.
   ![image.webp](5.webp){.img1}

5. Secure to the short aluminum profile, pay attention to the position of the 20-type M3 T-nut, it should be properly seated within the groove of the short profile.
   ![image.webp](6.webp){.img1}

!!! info "info"
    Do not overtighten to avoid damaging the printed parts.

6. The installation methods for the other two groups are the same.
   ![image.webp](7.webp){.img1}

???- faq "FAQ"

    **Q: What is the function of a CPAP fan?**


    **A:** The use of CPAP fan has large air volume, low frequency wind noise, constant air pressure, more stable heat dissipation, clean air inlet environment, longer life, and because the fan air intake is not in the cavity, it will not be affected by the temperature of the cavity, and there will be no vibration and no interference with the print head without a fan, and at the same time, the weight of the hanging table will be further reduced to meet the needs of high-speed printing.
