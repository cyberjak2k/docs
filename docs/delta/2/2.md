## 0.Precautions

!!! warning "warning"
    When fastening the printed parts with screws，do not tighten them too much to avoid damaging the printed parts.


!!! info "info"
    Printed parts throughout this section make use of threaded inserts, these need to be pressed into the printed part with a soldering iron:
    ![image.webp](1.webp){.img1}

### Assembly process

1.Screw the M5*16 cap-head screw through the foot and into the the lower skirt foot.
   ![image.webp](2.webp){.img1}

2.Screw the M3*10 cap-head screw into the lower skirt foot and fit the t-nut type 20-M3.
   ![image.webp](3.webp){.img1}

3.Simply screw on the M3*10 cap head screw; no need to tighten it completely.
   ![image.webp](4.webp){.img1}

4.Secure to the short aluminum profile.
   ![image.webp](5.webp){.img1}

5.Pay attention to the position ofthe t-nut, type 20-M3. It should be secured to the profile, but do not overtighten it to avoid damaging the lower skirt foot part.
   ![image.webp](6.webp){.img1}

6.The installation methods for the other two groups are the same.

## 7.Electrical fixtures

### Required materials

1.Bottom fixing plate * 1
   ![image.webp](36.webp){.img1}

2.Solid State Relay * 1
   ![image.webp](jdq.webp){.img1}

3.Switching power supply * 1
   ![image.webp](dy.webp){.img1}
   
4.M3*8 countersunk screws * 18
   ![image.webp](M3X8.webp){.img1}

5.M3*10 cap head screws * 2
   ![image.webp](M3X10.webp){.img1}

6.M3 self-locking nuts * 2
   ![image.webp](zslm.webp){.img1}

### Assembly process

1.Place the switching power supply on the bottom mounting plate, as shown in the figure.
   ![image.webp](37.webp){.img1}
   ![image.webp](38.webp){.img1}

2.Turn over and secure the the switching power supply with four M3*8 countersunk screws, as shown in the picture.
   ![image.webp](39.webp){.img1}

3.Place the solid-state relay onto the bottom mounting plate, as shown in the figure.
   ![image.webp](40.webp){.img1}

4.Note the markings on the solid-state relay, as shown in the figure.
   ![image.webp](41.webp){.img1}

5.Use two M3*10 cap-head screws to pass through the screw holes on the solid-state relay.
   ![image.webp](42.webp){.img1}
      
6.Turn over and secure with two M3 self-locking nuts.
   ![image.webp](43.webp){.img1}
   
7.Secure to the bottom using twelve M3*8 countersunk screws.
   ![image.webp](44.webp){.img1}

8.After completion, turn the printer over. Note that the touchscreen should be facing forward, and the power switch and the solid-state relay should be on the front right side.
   ![image.webp](45.webp){.img1}

???- question "FAQ"

    **Q: Is the skirt compatible with Voron?**

    **A:** Yes, skirt prints are compatible with Voron 3D printers and can also be installed on other Voron-style open-source 3D printers.


    **Q: Can the touch screen be installed on the Voron?**

    **A:** Yes, the touch screen is compatible with Voron 3D printers and can also be installed to any other 3D printer that uses 20 aluminum profiles.


    **Q:  Will shaking during printing under the frame affect the printing accuracy?**


    **A:** No, the rigid skeleton is load-bearing, flexible, easy to replace parts as function and mounting surface. In the high-speed, high-temperature, high-acceleration scenario, stability, accuracy, maintainability is comprehensively superior. To avoid long-term stress creep, high-temperature softening of the printed part can to lead to frame instability, flatness deterioration, metal frame high-strength connection to ensure high temperature, vibration almost never loosened. The printed part can be shock absorption, noise reduction, no resonance, to avoid the problems of high-frequency vibrous, lamination, corner drawing caused by direct hard contact of metal. At the same time, the printed parts have a high degree of freedom, can be expanded and modified, and all printed parts can be printed by the machine itself. Once setup, it might be good to print all printed parts, so you have spares incase things wear and you don't have another printer.
